About the University

SDU University is one of the leading educational institutions in Kazakhstan, known for its strong academic programs, modern infrastructure, and international learning environment.

Suleyman Demirel University

Established in 1996

SDU University provides high-quality education in IT, Business, Education, and Social Sciences. With modern laboratories, strong research focus, and highly-skilled faculty, SDU prepares students for global careers and future innovation. The university emphasizes critical thinking, leadership, and hands-on project experience.
